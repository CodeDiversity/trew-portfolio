Projects

Rate My Diy

Rate My DIY was a project that I was able to work on a team during Lambda School with. Myself and two other developers collaborated as a team to work on this project. We used React JS and Apollo Client on the front-end and we used GraphQL, and Apollo Server and Prisma for our backend. The goal of this app is to be a website where users can create an account, and share their favorite DIY home projects, other users can either up vote, or down vote the project and leave reviews.
